# dtDisplay – Clock Plugin for KOReader

> **dtDisplay** is a fullscreen clock plugin for KOReader. Instantly turn your Kindle or Kobo into a clean, always-on e-ink desk or bedside clock.

## Download

  - [dtDisplay on GitHub](https://github.com/kktse/dtdisplay.koplugin)

## Features

  - Fullscreen time and date for easy viewing
  - Perfect while your device is charging or at your desk
  - Designed specifically for e-ink screens
  - Tap to exit and return to KOReader instantly

## Installation

  1. Download from [dtDisplay on GitHub](https://github.com/kktse/dtdisplay.koplugin).
  2. Unzip or clone the repository.
  3. Copy the folder to `/koreader/plugins/` on your device.
  4. Restart KOReader.

## How to Use

  1. Open KOReader.
  2. Go to **Plugins → More tools → Time & Day**.
  3. The fullscreen clock appears. Tap the screen to close.
