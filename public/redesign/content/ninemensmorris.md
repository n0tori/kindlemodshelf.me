# Nine Men’s Morris

> Enjoy the classic Nine Men’s Morris board game on your Kindle! Choose either the **KUAL (softfloat)** app or the **KTerm (hardfloat)** terminal-launched version.

## Download

  - **SF:** [MobileRead: Nine Men's Morris (SF/KUAL)](https://www.mobileread.com/forums/showpost.php?p=2524556&postcount=43)
  - **HF:** [morris-kindle.zip](../downloads/morris-kindle.zip)

---

Softfloat (SF): KUAL App

**Who:** For jailbroken Kindles with softfloat firmware and KUAL installed.

## How to Install (SF/KUAL)

  1. **Download:** [MobileRead: Nine Men’s Morris (SF/KUAL)](https://www.mobileread.com/forums/showpost.php?p=2524556&postcount=43)
  2. Unzip and place the extension in `/mnt/us/extensions/` on your Kindle.
  3. Open KUAL — Nine Men’s Morris will appear in your menu.

## How to Play (SF/KUAL)

Launch Nine Men’s Morris directly from the KUAL menu.

## Notes (SF/KUAL)

  - Menu-driven, classic gameplay. Perfect for e-ink!

Hardfloat (HF): KTerm Version

**Who:** For jailbroken Kindles with hardfloat firmware and the KTerm terminal emulator.

## How to Install (HF/KTerm)

  1. **Download:** [morris-kindle.zip](../downloads/morris-kindle.zip)
  2. Unzip and copy the files to your Kindle.

## How to Play (HF/KTerm)

  1. Open KTerm and navigate (`cd`) to the game directory.
  2. Run the game with `./morris-kindle`.

## Notes (HF/KTerm)

  - **Disclaimer:** The hardfloat (HF) version is known to be unstable on many devices — it often crashes and was not made as robustly as the softfloat/KUAL version. Use the HF build with caution and prefer the KUAL release if you want a more reliable experience.
