# Screeny

> **Screeny** is a safe custom screensaver hack for jailbroken Kindles that works outside of KOReader without risking your device.

## Download

  - [Screeny on GitHub](https://github.com/KindleTweaks/Screeny)

## Features

  - Set custom screensavers outside of KOReader
  - Safe implementation: unlike NiLuJe's Hacks or manually replacing images, Screeny will not brick your device
  - No dangerous file replacements required
