# 2048 for Kindle

> **2048** is the number-merging puzzle where you combine tiles to reach 2048. Built with the Illusion framework and tuned for Kindle's e-ink display.

## Download

  - [Download 2048.zip](../downloads/2048.zip)

## Features

  - Classic 2048 on a 4×4 grid
  - **Arrow-button controls** (no swipe required)
  - Score and high-score tracking
  - Clean e-ink-friendly interface
  - Endless play—continue past 2048 if you want

## Requirements

  - Jailbroken Kindle with Mesquite support
  - Illusion framework compatibility

## How to Install

  1. Download [2048.zip](../downloads/2048.zip)
  2. Unzip the file on your computer (uncompressed folder + **.sh** script)
  3. Place the **.sh script** and the **folder** in your Kindle’s **documents** folder
  4. Disconnect your Kindle
  5. Tap the new book in your Kindle library to launch

## How to Play

  - Use the on-screen arrow buttons (▲ ◀ ▶ ▼) to slide the board
  - Equal tiles that collide merge into one (e.g., 2+2→4)
  - Keep merging tiles to create 2048
  - The game ends when no moves remain

Enjoy the 2048 puzzle on your Kindle's e-ink display!
