PaperCut

PaperCut is a jailbreak and mods installer app for Kindle.

Overview

Developed by Huckle, PaperCut serves as a utility for installing jailbreaks and modifications on your device. It functions similarly to other installer tools but offers a distinct approach for managing your Kindle's hacks.
