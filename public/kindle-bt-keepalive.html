Kindle BT Keepalive

A background utility to maintain active Bluetooth connections.

Download

  • Download Kindle-bt-keepalive V1.0.0
  • View Repository

Prerequisites

  • Jailbroken Kindle
  • Bluetooth-capable Kindle model
  • KUAL or SSH access for installation

Functions and Notes

  • Prevents the Kindle from putting the Bluetooth stack to sleep during long reading intervals.
  • Designed to let you focus entirely on your book, without the constant worry of your headphones turning off.
  • Lightweight script designed to have minimal impact on battery life.

Installation involves deploying the script to your extensions folder or running it via terminal. Refer to the README in the repository for detailed steps.
