# TicTacToe

> Classic TicTacToe for Kindle launchable from KUAL.
>  Install the extension to play quick matches on your jailbroken device.

## Download

[**TicTacToe**](https://github.com/progzone122/tictactoe-kindle/releases)

## Requirements

  - KUAL

## Instructions

  - Download from [TicTacToe](https://github.com/progzone122/tictactoe-kindle/releases).
  - Unzip the archive and move the `tictactoe` directory to the `extensions` folder on your Kindle.
  - Open KUAL and launch TicTacToe.
